How CalFresh Helps

CalFresh helps people get back on their feet

In California, a state with one of the highest costs of living in the country, nearly one in five people live in poverty. CalFresh helps millions of California workers in low-paying jobs buy healthy food during a time of need. CalFresh doesn't only help an individual make it through a difficult time, but also energizes the local economy.

In California...

27.3% lost a job in the last 30 days
65% of the rest are employed
80.9% who have a job work at least 20 hours a week
17.2% have a job that gives them 40+ hours per week
Source: CBPP
